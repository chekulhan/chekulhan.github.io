Negotiable Certificate Deposit (NCD) System

Written in the Prolog programming language using

An investment in an underlying security being a negotiable certificate of deposit (NCD) where the term of the security is generally for a period of 1 day to 2 years. The underlying security is issued by a bank. The interest rate applicable on an NCD is determined on the deal date by reference to prevailing market interest rates and is fixed for the term of the investment.
An NCD entitles the person in possession of it to payment, by the issuing bank, of a specified sum of money (face value) on a particular date (maturity date).
